Broughton High School PTFA 100 Club

Join the next PTFA 100 Club to support the school and win amazing monthly prizes!

Your participation benefits school projects and gives you a chance to win cash prizes.

How It Works?

  • Number of Draws: There will be 12 draws, 1 each month (Sep - Aug).
  • Subscription: Each draw costs £2. Annual membership will therefore cost £24 per number.
  • Number of Entries: You may purchase up to 3 numbers per person.
  • Allocation of Funds: 60% supports PTFA projects; 40% goes to prizes.
  • Prize Breakdown: Drawn monthly. First prize gets 50%, second prize 25%, third prize 15%, and 10% covers costs such as card fees etc.
  • Your Number: You will be randomly allocated a unique number, which will be your identifier in every draw.
